Our five week program focuses on the basic elements of the Christian life

Week 1: The Mystery of Human Life

Have you ever wondered why you are living in this world and what the purpose of your life is? There are six keys that unlock this mystery.

Week 2: The Assurance Security and Joy of Salvation

If you recently have had the experience of receiving Christ, you may have had times when you questioned whether you were really saved.

Without the real assurance of salvation as a solid foundation, it is difficult for a new Christian to grow and experience the deeper things of the Christian life.

The Bible says it is possible to know absolutely unreservedly, that you are a saved person. How can this be?

Week 3: The Precious Blood of Christ

To sustain your physical life, you need certain basic items such as water, oxygen, food, clothing, and shelter. It is the same with your spiritual life.

Your spiritual life, just like your physical life, requires certain basic elements. These are essential.

One of these basic elements is the blood of Christ. Why do you need the blood of Christ?

Week 4: Calling on the Name of the Lord

What is the meaning of calling on the name of the Lord? Why do we need to call on the name of the Lord? How should we call on the Lord?
